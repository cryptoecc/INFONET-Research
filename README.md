# INFONET-Research

Open research on AI, intelligent sensing, and hyperspectral imaging systems.

---

## About

INFONET-Research is an open research organization focused on advancing:

- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Intelligent Sensing Systems
- Hyperspectral Imaging (HSI)
- Foundation Models & LLMs
- AI Agents & Multi-Agent Systems
- Signal Processing & Machine Learning
- Efficient AI Systems

We develop reproducible research, open-source implementations, datasets, and scalable AI systems for real-world intelligent sensing and AI applications.

---

## Research Areas

### AI Systems & Foundation Models
Research on scalable transformer architectures, efficient training, and large language models.

### Intelligent Sensing
Machine learning methods for sensor intelligence, signal understanding, and real-world perception systems.

### Hyperspectral Imaging (HSI)
Deep learning and signal processing techniques for hyperspectral image classification, restoration, super-resolution, and representation learning.

### AI Agents & Multi-Agent Systems
Autonomous AI agents, collaborative reasoning systems, and agent-based intelligence.

### Signal Processing & Machine Learning
Advanced signal processing algorithms integrated with modern machine learning frameworks.

## Research Vision

We aim to bridge:

- AI and sensing technologies
- Foundation models and real-world systems
- Signal processing and modern deep learning
- Scalable research and practical deployment

Our goal is to build trustworthy, efficient, and intelligent AI systems for future sensing and perception environments.
